S. P.Balasubrahmanyam

Sripathi Panditaradhyula Balasubrahmanyam (4 June 1946 - 25 September 2020), also referred to as SPB or SP Balu or Balu, was an Indian playback singer, television presenter, actor, music composer, dubbing artist, and film producer. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest Indian singers of all time.He predominantly worked in Tamil ,Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, and Hindi films and sang in a total of 16 languages.
